In line vs Block Objects

My ah-ha moment with block elements vs in-line elements was this question: do you want a new line with this element or not? Although there is definitely more to the two elements than this fact, the difference between adding a new line or not greatly affects the design and layout of your elements. Further research taught me that block elements are actually created to hold other elements. For instance this text is held inside of a <p> tag. This detail also greatly helps with designing your page. It is good to think about elements as a hierarchy. When adding an element ask yourself is this element going to hold other elements and objects? If not is it being held by a parent element?
